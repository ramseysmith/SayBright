SayBright Support

SayBright is a daily affirmations app designed to help you build a positive mindset through consistent practice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?
Go to Settings, then tap Restore Purchases. Your premium features will be reactivated.
How do streaks work?
Open SayBright once each day to maintain your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets to 1.
How do I add the home screen widget?
Long press your home screen, tap the plus button in the top left, and search for SayBright. Widgets are available for premium subscribers.
How do I change my affirmation categories?
Go to Settings and tap Categories to choose which affirmation topics appear in your daily feed.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions to manage or cancel.
