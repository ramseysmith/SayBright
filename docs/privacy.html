SayBright Privacy Policy

Effective May 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. SayBright stores your preferences, favorites, and streak data locally on your device. Anonymous usage data may be collected through AdMob (Google) for ad serving purposes. Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, which processes transactions through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat may collect anonymous identifiers tied to your device.

2. Advertising

The free version of SayBright displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements.

3. Subscriptions

SayBright offers auto renewable subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Payment is processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID settings at any time.

4. Data Storage

All app data (favorites, streaks, preferences, daily usage counts) is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard secure storage. We do not transmit your affirmation preferences or personal data to any server we operate.

5. Children's Privacy

SayBright is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can investigate.

6. Tracking Transparency

On iOS devices, SayBright requests permission via App Tracking Transparency before any third party (such as AdMob) can use device identifiers for tracking. If you decline, you will continue to see ads, but they will not be personalized.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page along with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at support@saybright.app.
